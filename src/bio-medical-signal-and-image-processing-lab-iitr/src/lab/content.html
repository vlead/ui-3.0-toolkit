Introduction

Welcome to the Bio-Medical Signal and Image Processing Lab developed at IIT Roorkee. The interactive experiments in this lab will give the students an opportunity for learning and better understanding of Bio-Medical Signal Processing and basics of the techniques used in Bio-Medcical Image Processing

List of experiments
  • Digital Signal Processing Toolkit
  • Bio-Medical Signal Processing Toolkit
  • Image Processing Toolkit
Target Audience

The experiments are targeted at students who have just finished a first course in Digital Processing and Image Processing.

Courses Aligned

The present lab is aligned with courses such as Digital Signal Processing, Bio-medical Signal Processing, and the Image processing. The experiments touch on most topics covered in such courses in most curricula.

Prerequisite S/W

  • Java Runtime Environment allows you to play online virtual labs.
  • LabVIEW 2013 Run-Time Engine
